The team behind Sticky Password

Sticky Password was founded in 2001 by Alex (bottom row, left), shown with the sales and marketing team in our home city of Brno, Czech Republic. As a programmer struggling to manage his growing list of passwords, Alex built a tool for himself—one that quickly became essential for his friends and family, too. What began as a imple Windows solution in a local town has since grown into a global password manager supporting Windows, Android, iOS, and Mac, used by customers in almost every country around the world.
