Contactless Connect is the new unique way of logging in to websites on computers without having to install the Sticky Password application on the device!

 
Join the Beta test
 

To create a smart connection between Sticky Password on your smartphone and any device with a browser, simply

  1. click Sticky Password’s extension button in the browser on the computer to generate a unique QR code,
  2. and tap the built-in QR code scanner in the Sticky Password app on your smartphone to scan the QR code.

No typing or navigating needed! Just select the account from the Sticky Password database on your smartphone and watch as Sticky Password opens the site on the computer and logs you in.

 
 

Ideal for:

Computers where installation of the Sticky Password application is not possible for technical reasons, such as Linux and Chromebooks.
Environments where shared computers make it impractical to install the Sticky Password application for a single user.
Environments where installation of outside applications is restricted by office policy.
Remote desktops, virtual desktops, and kiosk mode.
IT Crowd-types in the office who go from desk to desk sorting their colleagues’ computer tech issues will enjoy the super-convenient login.
And, Contactless Connect is a convenient cross-platform solution for assisting friends and relatives who use a different computer platform than you do.
 
 

To give the new Contactless Connect a test run, make sure you have installed the latest beta version of Sticky Password and browser extension. https://beta.stickypassword.com/contactless
