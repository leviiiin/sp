We know that security and efficiency are key to your business—whether you're working in the office, providing remote support via TeamViewer, or assisting customers onsite.

That’s why we’re excited to introduce Contactless Connect, a powerful new feature in Sticky Password that takes password security to the next level.

 
 

Why You’ll Want to Use It

Passwords aren’t going away anytime soon, but how we use them is evolving.

Passkeys are gaining traction as a "passwordless" login option, but adoption remains limited, and passwords still require careful management. Contactless Connect ensures versatile and secure access to passwords across Windows, macOS, Linux, ChromeOS, virtual environments, remote desktops, and Remote Browser Isolation solutions - environments with strict security controls.

With Contactless Connect, logging in is easier and more secure than ever—no installations, no password database copies everywhere. Users simply scan a QR code with their smartphone to authenticate, and their passwords are securely filled when and where needed, always staying end-to-end encrypted.

✔ No more typing passwords manually—just scan a QR code to log in.
✔ Perfect for IT teams assisting colleagues—quickly log in while helping co-workers without saving passwords on shared computers.
✔ Ideal for customer site visits—log in securely without leaving credentials behind.
✔ Seamless for remote work, RBI, and remote access environments—giving you more flexibility to assist customers securely from anywhere.
✔ Works across more platforms and even secure browsers—log in seamlessly on Windows, macOS, Linux, and ChromeOS, even in incognito or private browsing mode.
