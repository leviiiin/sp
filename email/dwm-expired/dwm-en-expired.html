Frequently asked questions

Q. Won’t my passwords be exposed on the internet during the scan?

A. No. Thanks to the customized solution we`ve implemented, your passwords never leave your device during the scan. All verification is performed directly on your device ensuring your passwords always remain secure and private.

Q. Doesn’t my password manager protect me against data breaches?

A. Sticky Password helps protect you against data breaches by enabling you to have strong unique passwords for each account which are protected in your encrypted storage on your devices and also backed up in our secure cloud servers.

Q. What does it mean when my logins and passwords have been found on the dark web?

A. It means that your passwords are included in the breach data that “bad actors” (hackers and cybercriminals) are buying and selling on the internet.

Q. What should I do if my data is found in breach data?

A. An exposed password doesn’t mean that your account has necessarily been compromised - though that is possible. By taking quick action to change passwords of the affected accounts, checking for unauthorized activity, etc. you can mitigate the risk to your accounts - often, even before your accounts have been violated.
